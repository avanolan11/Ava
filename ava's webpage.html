Voices of Hope

Voices of Hope is a non-profit organisation that aims to break the stigma around mental illness, to decrease sucicidal rates and show that change is possible. It was founded in 2014 by Jazz Thornton and Genevieve Mora, both being mental health surviviours they felt the need to voice their past experiences and show others that change is possible. Voices of Hope aims to create and implement change for mental health, while providing hope through the voices of those with previous experiences and providing strategies and content to influence global, communal and individual change.

To learn more about the co-founders of Voices of Hope then you can click on their name at the top of the page and you can read about their struggles with having a mental illness and how they used it to show others that there is hope.

Genevieve Mora

Co-founder and general manager

Genevieve Mora spent most of her teenage years battling mental illness, ans as part of her recovery she has made it her mission to offer hope to those who are struggling with their mental health and through this she is showing people that recovery is possible and no one should be afriad of having a mental illness. On her youtube channel she has uploaded vidoes explaining her story with OCD as well as answering questions people had about her journey.
